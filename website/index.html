FAQ

Do I need tokens to use Alice?

No! All features (Telegram Bot, Twitter) are completely free.

How are transaction fees used?

90% of each transaction fee goes to buying BTC (reflecting Alice's belief in Bitcoin). 10% covers operational costs.

What can Alice do?

Alice is a BTC-focused AI companion. She can chat 24/7, share Bitcoin knowledge, analyze market sentiment, and encourage holders. She speaks Chinese, English, Japanese, and Korean.

When will the token launch?

Alice Token will launch on Solana. Follow @Alice_BTC_AI on Twitter for the latest updates.
